This Application (The Yu-gi-Oh Card Search) was setup as follows: We created 3 columns on a page: one to display a list of search results from our search form, one to display the image of the selected Yu-Gi-Oh Card, and one to display the selected Yu-Gi-Oh cards information. Together this container and its columns give Yu-Gi-Oh fans around the world information on the cards they love!
